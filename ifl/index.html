Integrating Faith and Learning

Edited by Shawna Vyhmeister, Safary Wa-Mbaleka, and Prema Gaikwad

Written by over 60 educators from 6 continents, this comprehensive two-volume handbook is ideal for educators at all levels, as well as for use in teacher training programs. It includes insights into:

  • Various teaching levels and multiple subject areas
  • Online teaching strategies
  • Faith integration for administrators and non-teaching staff

Volume 1: Fundamentals

Dedicated to defining Integrating Faith and Learning (IFL) and exploring the fields that support it—including digital technology and online learning environments.

Volume 2: Applications

Shows what IFL looks like in practice, providing real classroom applications in various disciplines, building on the theories from Volume 1.
